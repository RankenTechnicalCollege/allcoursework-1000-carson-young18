Members

Albums

Bouquet
Their very first EP, Bouquet followed up on the group's hit single 'Selfie'. The very first song, 'Roses', very quickly took over popular radio channels and charts.
Memories...Do Not Open
Memories...Do Not Open was their debut studio album, released in April 2017. Two of the tracks, 'Paris' and 'Something Just Like This' had extended stays in multiple national top 10 lists.
Collage
Collage was the group's second EP. It consists of 5 songs, including the song 'Closer' which dominated the radio and has amassed over 1 billion streams on Spotify.
